## 4) Money Flow Guarantee

ADAM's only purpose in money is to move it as directly as possible from listener to artist.
This section locks how that happens and who is never allowed to touch it.

### Core Principle

Money is the artist's. ADAM's job is to route it there, not manage it, hold it, or skim it.

There is no ADAM middleman. There is no ADAM fee. There is no ADAM value-extraction layer.

Every dollar that reaches an artist because of ADAM routing is 100% the artist's.

### Direct-to-Fan Only (MVP)

In MVP, ADAM enables money flows only through:
- Direct support (tips, one-time payments)
- Artist-controlled platforms (Bandcamp, Patreon, artist's own store)
- Instant payment systems (Stripe, PayPal, direct bank transfer)
- No marketplace, no aggregator, no platform middle tier

If a payment rail exists that lets the listener pay the artist directly (without platform skim),
ADAM uses that route first.

If a route requires a platform middleman, ADAM:
- Discloses the fee upfront
- Makes alternative direct routes visible
- Defaults to the artist's choice

### Transparency Requirement (Non-Negotiable)

Before any money path is enabled, the artist sees:
- Where the money comes from
- How much the artist receives
- Fees (if any)
- Settlement timeline
- How to access/withdraw

No hidden fees.
No margin compression.
No "processing costs."

If ADAM cannot make this transparent, ADAM does not enable that route.

### Settlement Speed

Artists should earn money the same day their work is routed (or within 24 hours).

Not "weekly."
Not "monthly."
Not "pending review."

Same day.

This is not aspirational. This is a requirement for MVP.

### Undo / Refund Rights

If a listener disputes a support payment or requests a refund:
- Artist is notified
- Refund is processed
- Artist is not penalized
- Artist is not surprised by chargebacks

The artist controls the final decision on refunds.
ADAM does not hold money hostage during disputes.

### The "Even-If" Hard Stop

Even if an investor says "we could take a small fee," ADAM will not:
- Take a percentage of artist earnings
- Hold money longer than 24 hours
- Require artists to opt into payment delays for better rates
- Charge artists for access to their own money
- Bundle artist data with payment processing
- Use payment data for targeting or optimization

These are architectural, not negotiable.

### Binding Statement

Money flows from listener to artist as directly as possible with zero ADAM intermediation.
Settlement is same-day or next-business-day. All fees are disclosed upfront. ADAM takes nothing.
The artist always owns their money and the right to receive it immediately.

OPTION B (Speed & Certainty-First)

Emphasis: Guaranteed same-day settlement, no delays, no surprises, predictable timelines.

================================================================================

## 4) Money Flow Guarantee

ADAM guarantees that artists know exactly how much money they earned, how fast they'll receive it,
and that no delays, holds, or surprises will interfere with livelihood.

### The Settlement Promise

When a listener pays through an ADAM-routed surface:

1. Artist is notified in real-time
2. Amount is confirmed (what the artist receives, not gross)
3. Settlement timeline is stated upfront
4. Money reaches artist by promised date or ADAM compensates for delay

No "pending" states.
No "processing."
No indefinite holds.

### Settlement Timeline (Locked)

- Same-day routes: Settlement within 24 hours
- Bank transfer routes: Next business day
- Special routes: Disclosed timeline upfront, honored always

If a payment route cannot meet this timeline, ADAM does not use it.

### Fee Transparency (Pre-Action)

Before any payment method is enabled, the artist sees:

"If a listener pays $10:
- Platform fee: $1.00
- Payment processor fee: $0.50
- You receive: $8.50
- Settlement: Tomorrow by 2pm"

This is shown before any payment is collected.
Artist approves or selects a different route.

No surprises. Ever.

### Artist Control of Money Paths

The artist can:
- Choose which payment methods are enabled
- Change methods anytime
- Disable a method immediately
- Set minimum payout thresholds (or not)
- Withdraw anytime (no waiting periods)

ADAM does not lock money in escrow.
ADAM does not require batch payouts.
Artist withdraws when they decide.

### Real-Time Accounting

Artist can see at any time:
- Total earned today
- Total earned this week/month
- What's pending
- What's settled
- Detailed transaction log
- Estimated next payout

No guessing.
No mystery delays.
Full visibility.

### Dispute & Refund Handling

If a listener disputes or refunds a payment:
1. Artist is notified immediately
2. Amount is deducted from next payout (or adjusted in real-time accounting)
3. Artist is not penalized for the dispute
4. Artist can appeal if fraudulent dispute

Artist is never surprised by chargeback or held liable for listener disputes.

### The "Even-If" Hard Stop

Even if a payment processor requests longer holds, ADAM will not:
- Delay settlement beyond stated timeline
- Hold money in ADAM accounts longer than necessary
- Charge artists for faster payouts (i.e., no "premium settlement" fees)
- Require batch minimums
- Restrict withdrawal frequency
- Use payment delays as leverage for anything

Speed and certainty are not features. They are requirements.

### Binding Statement

ADAM guarantees same-day or next-business-day settlement, full fee transparency pre-transaction,
and zero arbitrary holds or delays. Artist withdrawals are immediate and unrestricted.
Real-time accounting is always visible. Money moves predictably or ADAM compensates.

## 4) Money Flow Guarantee

ADAM does not own the money flow. The artist does.

ADAM's role is to present options, execute the artist's choice, and stay out of the way.

### Artist Sovereignty Over Money

The artist:
- Chooses which payment methods are available
- Owns the payment account
- Controls settlement frequency
- Decides refund policies
- Can audit ADAM's handling of money flows
- Can exit any money path without penalty

ADAM is the executor of artist intent, not the architect.

### Money Path Options (Artist Chooses)

ADAM can enable multiple parallel money paths:
- Direct support (Stripe, PayPal, bank transfer)
- Artist's own Bandcamp/Patreon account
- Cryptocurrency or alternative payment systems
- Institutional or DIY options

Artist chooses which are enabled.
Artist can add/remove at any time.
Artist sees fees for each upfront.

### Radical Transparency

Before any transaction:
- What the listener pays
- What the artist receives (net of all fees)
- Where the money goes
- Settlement timeline
- How to track it

Artist approves the flow or chooses a different route.

### Artist Controls Settlement Terms

Artist can decide:
- Payout frequency (daily, weekly, or manual withdrawal)
- Minimum payout thresholds (or none)
- Which accounts receive money
- Whether to batch or stream payouts

No ADAM defaults.
Artist chooses.

### Full Audit Rights

Artist can see:
- Every transaction in detail
- Every fee charged
- Every hold or delay (with reason)
- Communication from payment processors
- ADAM's own ledger

If ADAM cannot explain a fee or delay, artist can challenge it.
Challenge is binding.

### The "Even-If" Hard Stop

Even if it's more efficient or scalable, ADAM will not:
- Override artist's payment method choice
- Hold money longer than artist agreed
- Take fees without explicit artist approval for each transaction type
- Combine artist data with payment processing
- Use payment information for any purpose other than settlement
- Restrict artist's ability to move money to competing platforms
- Penalize artists for exiting a payment path

Artist sovereignty over money is non-negotiable.

### Binding Statement

Artist owns the money path and decides all terms. ADAM executes artist choices transparently.
Every fee is disclosed. Every settlement timeline is honored. Artist has full audit rights and
can change or exit any payment method anytime without penalty. Money belongs to the artist.
Always.
